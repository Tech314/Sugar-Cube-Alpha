Sugar Cube Alpha

About the game

Sugar Cube Alpha is a pong style game, where the objective is to use your paddle to block a sugar cube from infiltrating your morning coffee

Instructions

  • To Start/Pause/Unpause the game - press 'Shift'
  • First player (left) controls - Press 'W' to move up and 'S' to move down
  • Second player (right) controls - Press 'I' to move up and 'K' to move down
  • To reset the game - press 'R'
